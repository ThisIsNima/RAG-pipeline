TITLE: Ambidextrous Organizations: Managing Evolutionary and Revolutionary Change
YEAR: 1996
TOPIC: art
OPENALEX_ID: https://openalex.org/W2023596193

Organizations evolve through periods of incremental or evolutionary change punctuated by discontinuous or revolutionary change. The challenge for managers is to adapt the culture and strategy of their organizations to its current environment, but to do so in a way that does not undermine its ability to adjust to radical changes in that environment.
