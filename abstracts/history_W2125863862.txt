TITLE: Reactive Species and Antioxidants. Redox Biology Is a Fundamental Theme of Aerobic Life
YEAR: 2006
TOPIC: history
OPENALEX_ID: https://openalex.org/W2125863862

The field of antioxidants and free radicals is often perceived as focusing around the use of antioxidant supplements to prevent human disease. In fact, antioxidants/free radicals permeate the whole of life, creating the field of redox biology. Free radicals are not all bad, nor antioxidants all good
