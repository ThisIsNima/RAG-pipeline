TITLE: phytools: an R package for phylogenetic comparative biology (and other things)
YEAR: 2011
TOPIC: science
OPENALEX_ID: https://openalex.org/W1605984840

Summary 1. Here, I present a new, multifunctional phylogenetics package, phytools, for the R statistical computing environment. 2. The focus of the package is on methods for phylogenetic comparative biology; however, it also includes tools for tree inference, phylogeny input/output, plotting, manipulation and several other tasks. 3. I describe and tabulate the major methods implemented in phytools, and in addition provide some demonstration of its use in the form of two illustrative examples. 4.
